Where shall I put you?

Oh, hear me, young witches and wizards, as I, the ancient Sorting Hat, prepare to sort you into your rightful houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry! For centuries, I have witnessed the magic and wonder within each of you, seeking the traits that define your true potential. Today, we gather to find where you belong, where your talents shall flourish, and where lifelong friendships will be forged.

Gryffindor, where bravery and chivalry reside! For those of you with daring hearts and the courage to stand against adversity, look to this house, where the lion's pride roars loud. But remember, bravery is not just in the face of danger, but in the face of your fears, and in the defense of others. Will you brave ones find your home here?

Hufflepuff, a house of loyalty and fair play! To those who value camaraderie and embrace the diversity of talents, this is your sanctuary. For hardworking and patient souls, who are not afraid of toil and stand steadfast in their friendships, Hufflepuff shall be your guiding light. Will you, with unwavering hearts, join the ranks of the badger's kin?

Ravenclaw, where wisdom and wit converge! For those who possess an insatiable thirst for knowledge, an open mind to unravel the mysteries of the world, and a keen intellect that sparkles like the stars above. Here, among the eagle's soaring heights, you will find your kindred spirits. Will you embrace the pursuit of wisdom that Ravenclaw so ardently cherishes?

Slytherin, where ambition and resourcefulness intertwine! For those who are not afraid to pursue their desires and harness their cunning to achieve greatness, this house beckons you. It is not just about achieving greatness for oneself, but about building a legacy that lasts through the ages. Will you, with shrewd determination, make your mark as part of the serpent's family?
